Social Media Checklist

The Do’s (Smart Practices):

Protect Your Privacy:
Review your account settings and keep your personal information restricted. Allow only people you trust to access your private profiles.

Create a Strong Digital Portfolio:
Share your achievements, such as completed projects, certifications, or skills you’ve learned. This helps build a positive online image.

Regular Self-Check:
Search your name online from time to time to see what others can find about you. Remove or update anything that may not reflect you well.

Think Before You Post:
If you feel unsure or emotional while posting, pause and review it later. This helps avoid posting something you might regret.

Network Professionally:
Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with professionals, follow industry leaders, and stay updated with career opportunities.


The Don’ts (Common Mistakes to Avoid):

Avoid Public Complaints:
Do not post negative comments about your college, workplace, teachers, or colleagues. It can harm your reputation.

Do Not Share Sensitive Information:
Avoid posting personal details such as ID cards, addresses, or daily routines, as it can lead to safety risks.

stay Away from Online Arguments:
Engaging in negative or toxic discussions online can damage your professional image.

Be Careful with Photos:
Avoid sharing inappropriate or unprofessional pictures. Always think about how it may be perceived by others.

Don’t Rely on Deleting Posts:
Even if you delete something, it might already be saved or shared by others. Always post responsibly.

This checklist helps maintain a safe, respectful, and professional presence on social media.
