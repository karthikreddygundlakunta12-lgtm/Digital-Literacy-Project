description:

I created my digital literacy infographic using Canva because it offers a simple and user-friendly way to design visuals.

In my infographic, I clearly explained the concept of digital literacy and highlighted its importance for students.

I covered key areas such as useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

I presented the information using concise points and a clean layout to ensure it is easy to understand at a glance.

The infographic demonstrates how students can effectively use tools like Google Docs, Gmail, and GitHub in their academic work.

It also emphasizes online safety by suggesting practices such as creating strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links.

Additionally, I included guidelines on maintaining a professional online profile and writing clear, polite emails.

While designing the infographic, I initially found it challenging to fit all the information into a single page without making it look crowded.
